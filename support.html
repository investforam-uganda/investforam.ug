Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ▼
Click "Create Account" on the homepage, fill in your details including full name, email, and phone number. You'll receive a verification email to complete registration.
What is the minimum investment? ▼
Minimum investment is UGX 300,000 for Basic Plan. Growth Plan requires UGX 1,000,000, Premium UGX 3,000,000, VIP UGX 10,000,000.
How do I make a deposit? ▼
Go to your dashboard, click "Deposit", select payment method, enter amount and phone number, then follow the instructions to complete payment.
How do I withdraw my money? ▼
Navigate to dashboard, click "Withdraw", select method, enter amount and account details. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours.
What are the investment returns? ▼
Basic: 8% monthly, Growth: 12% monthly, Premium: 25% quarterly, VIP: 50% annually.
How does referral system work? ▼
Share your unique referral code. When someone signs up using your code and makes their first deposit, you get UGX 5,000 credited to your account.
