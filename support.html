Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ▼
Click on "Create Account" on the homepage, fill in your details including full name, email, and phone number. You'll receive a verification code to complete registration.
What is the minimum investment amount? ▼
The minimum investment amount is UGX 300,000 for the Basic Plan. Other plans have higher minimums: Growth Plan (UGX 1,000,000), Premium Plan (UGX 3,000,000), and VIP Plan (UGX 10,000,000).
How do I make a deposit? ▼
Go to your dashboard, click "Deposit", select your payment method (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or Bank Transfer), enter your phone number and amount, then confirm. You'll receive a confirmation once processed.
How do I withdraw my money? ▼
Navigate to your dashboard, click "Withdraw", select your withdrawal method, enter your phone number/account details, and the amount. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours.
What are the investment returns? ▼
Returns vary by plan: Basic Plan (2% weekly), Standard Plan (5% bi-weekly), Growth Plan (12% monthly), Premium Plan (25% quarterly), VIP Plan (50% annually).
Is my money safe? ▼
Yes! We use bank-level security encryption, and all transactions are monitored. Your funds are held in secure accounts with top-tier financial institutions.
How does the referral system work? ▼
Share your unique referral code with friends. When they sign up using your code and make their first deposit, you'll receive UGX 5,000 credited to your account.
Can I change my investment plan? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. Contact support or make a new investment with a higher plan to upgrade automatically.
